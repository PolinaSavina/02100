What 02100 does

In a high-risk moment, many people don’t get fight-or-flight — they freeze. 02100 is designed to safely disrupt immobility using controlled EMS and brief cold exposure, helping users regain intentional movement.

Freeze interruption

Controlled EMS and brief cold stimulus designed to break immobility and restore voluntary movement.

Designed for automation

Biometrics and system logic reduce misfires, with fast cancel and manual override.

Safety-first engineering

Watchdog logic and conservative triggering built around uncertainty.

How it works

1) Wear

Bracelet designed for continuous skin contact and urban everyday use.

2) Detect

Heart-rate sensing (PPG) and system logic identify freeze-like states in real time.

3) Interrupt + regain control

EMS and cold-plate stimulation help disrupt immobility and transition the user back to action.
