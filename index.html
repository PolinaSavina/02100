FAQ

What problem are you solving?

In high-risk urban situations, some people experience a freeze response rather than fight-or-flight, delaying movement and decision-making when seconds matter. 02100 is designed to help interrupt this immobility using controlled physical stimuli and system automation, supporting a faster return to intentional action and situational awareness.

Is this a medical device?

02100 is a consumer personal-safety wearable. It is not a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, mitigate, or prevent any disease or medical condition.

What’s next?

Next milestones include finalizing the production-ready hardware design, expanding automation and safety logic, running structured user-testing protocols, developing the companion mobile application, and preparing manufacturing and supply-chain operations for launch.
